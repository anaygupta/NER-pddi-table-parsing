
               
               
               CLINICAL PHARMACOLOGY
               
                  Aminosyn II 8.5% WITH ELECTROLYTES, Sulfite-Free, (an amino acid injection with electrolytes) provides crystalline amino acids to promote protein synthesis and wound healing, and to reduce the rate of endogenous protein catabolism. Aminosyn II 8.5% WITH ELECTROLYTES, given by central venous infusion in combination with concentrated dextrose, electrolytes, vitamins, trace metals, and ancillary fat supplements, constitutes total parenteral nutrition (TPN). AminosynÂ II 8.5% WITH ELECTROLYTES can also be administered by peripheral vein with dextrose and maintenance electrolytes. Intravenous fat emulsion may be substituted for part of the carbohydrate calories during either TPN or peripheral vein administration of Aminosyn II 8.5% WITH ELECTROLYTES.
