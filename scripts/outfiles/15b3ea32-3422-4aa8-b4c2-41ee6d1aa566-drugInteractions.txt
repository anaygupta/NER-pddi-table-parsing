
                     
                     
                     Drug Interactions:
                     
                        The vasodilating effects of nitroglycerin may be additive with those of other vasodilators. Alcohol, in particular, has been found to exhibit additive effects of this variety.
                        Concomitant use of MINITRAN with phosphodiesterase inhibitors in any form is contraindicated (see CONTRAINDICATIONS).
                        Concomitant use of MINITRAN with riociguat, a soluble guanylate cyclase stimulator, is contraindicated (see CONTRAINDICATIONS).
