
                     
                     
                     Drug Interactions
                     
                        Additive adverse effects resulting from cholinergic blockade may occur when NuLev® is administered concomitantly with other antimuscarinics, amantadine, haloperidol, phenothiazines, monoamine oxidase (MAO) inhibitors, tricyclic antidepressants or some antihistamines.
                        Antacids may interfere with the absorption of hyoscyamine sulfate. Administer NuLev® before meals; antacids after meals.
