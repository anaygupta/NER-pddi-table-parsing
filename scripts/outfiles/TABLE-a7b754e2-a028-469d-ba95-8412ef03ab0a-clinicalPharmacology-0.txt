<sup>a</sup>   Organisms susceptible to tetracycline are also considered susceptible to doxycycline. However, some organisms that are intermediate or resistant to tetracycline may be susceptible to doxycycline.<br/>
<sup>b</sup>  The current absence of resistance isolates precludes defining any results other than “Susceptible”.  If isolates yielding MIC results other than susceptible, they should be submitted to a reference laboratory for further testing.<br/>
<sup>c </sup>  Gonococci with 30 mcg tetracycline disk zone diameters of &lt; 19 mm usually indicate a plasmid-mediated tetracycline resistant <content stylecode="italics">Neisseria gonorrhoeae </content>isolate.  Resistance in these strains should be confirmed by a dilution test (MIC ≥ 16 mcg/mL).<br/>
