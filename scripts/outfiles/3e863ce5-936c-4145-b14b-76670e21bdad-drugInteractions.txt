
                     
                     
                     Drug Interactions
                     
                        The CNS-depressant effects of Synalgos-DC may be additive with that of other CNS depressants. See “
                              WARNINGS
                           .” 
                        Aspirin may enhance the effects of anticoagulants and inhibit the uricosuric effects of uricosuric agents.
