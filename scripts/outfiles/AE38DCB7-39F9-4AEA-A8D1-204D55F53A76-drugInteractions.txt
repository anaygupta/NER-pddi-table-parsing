


Drug Interactions
Patient receiving other opioid analgesics, general anesthetics, phenothiazines, other tranquilizers, sedative-hypnotics or other CNS depressants (including alcohol) concomitantly with Oxycodone and Acetaminophen Tablets, USP may exhibit an additive CNS depression.  When such combined therapy is contemplated, the dose of one or both agents should be reduced.The concurrent use of anticholinergics with opioids may produce paralytic ileus.
