
                           
                           
                           
                              Drug Interactions
                           
                           
                              When co-administered with voriconazole (inhibitor of CYP2C9, 2C19 and 3A4  enzyme), the Cmax and AUC of diclofenac increased by 114% and 78%, respectively (see PRECAUTIONS, Drug Interactions).
