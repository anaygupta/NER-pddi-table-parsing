
               
               
               CLINICAL PHARMACOLOGY
               
                  Mechanism of Action
                  
COREG is a racemic mixture in which nonselectiveblocking activity is present in the S(-) enantiomer andblocking activity is present in both R(+) and S(-) enantiomers at equal potency. COREG has no intrinsic sympathomimetic activity.
