


Drug Interactions
Monoamine oxidase (MAO) inhibitors-Hyperpyrexia, hypotension, and death have been reported coincident with the coadministration of MAO inhibitors and products containing dextromethorphan. In addition, MAO inhibitors prolong and intensify the anticholinergic (drying) effects of antihistamines and may enhance the effect of pseudoephedrine. Concomitant administration of Myphetane DX Cough Syrup and MAO inhibitors should be avoided (see CONTRAINDICATIONS).CONTRAINDICATIONS).





Central nervous system (CNS) depressants
Antihistamines have additive effects with alcohol and other CNS depressants (hypnotics, sedatives, tranquilizers, antianxiety agents, etc.).







Antihypertensive drugs
Sympathomimetic may reduce the effects of antihypertensive drugs.
