
                     
                     
                     7. DRUG INTERACTIONS
                     
                     
                        
                           
                              
                                 Allopurinol: Avoid use (7.1)
                                 Warfarin: PURIXAN may inhibit the anticoagulant effect. (7.2)
                              
                           
                        
                     
                     
                        
                           
                           
                              
					Certain drugs have been shown to interact with PURIXAN through pharmacokinetic and pharmacodynamic mechanisms.  Interactions known to decrease the clearance of PURIXAN include inhibition of first-pass oxidative metabolism by xanthine oxidase and inhibition of thiopurine methyltransferase (TPMT).  
					Consult the labeling of all concurrently used drugs to obtain further information about drug interactions with PURIXAN.
