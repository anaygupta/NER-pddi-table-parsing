
                     
                     
                     Drug Interactions
                     
                        Patients receiving narcotics, antihistamines, antipsychotics, antianxiety agents, or other CNS depressants (including alcohol) concomitantly with hydrocodone bitartrate and acetaminophen tablets may exhibit an addictive CNS depression. When combined therapy is contemplated, the dose of one or both agents should be reduced.
                        The use of MAO inhibitors or tricyclic antidepressants with hydrocodone preparations may increase the effect of either the antidepressant or hydrocodone
