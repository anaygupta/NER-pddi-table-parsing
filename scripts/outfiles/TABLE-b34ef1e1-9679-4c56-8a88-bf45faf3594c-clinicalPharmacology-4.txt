<footnote>Susceptibility of <content stylecode="italics">S. pneumoniae</content> should be determined using a 1 mcg oxacillin disk. An amoxicillin and clavulanic acid MIC should be determined on isolates of <content stylecode="italics">S. pneumoniae</content> with oxacillin zone sizes of ≤19 mm.</footnote> and <content stylecode="italics">N. gonorrhoeae</content>
