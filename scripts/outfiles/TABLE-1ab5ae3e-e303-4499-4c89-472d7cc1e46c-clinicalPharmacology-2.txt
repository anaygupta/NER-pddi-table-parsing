<sup>§</sup>     Susceptibility interpretive criteria for <content stylecode="italics">Enterobacteriaceae </content>are based on a dose of 1 gram q8h. For isolates with intermediate susceptibility, use a dose of 2 grams every 8 hours in patients with normal renal function. <br/>*     For <content stylecode="italics">P. aeruginosa</content>, susceptibility interpretive criteria are based on a dose of 2 grams IV every 8 hours in patients with normal renal function. <br/>
<sup>a</sup>     The current absence of data on resistant isolates precludes defining any category other than ‘Susceptible’. If isolates yield MIC results other than susceptible, they should be submitted to a reference laboratory for additional testing. <br/>Susceptibility of staphylococci to ceftazidime may be deduced from testing only penicillin and either cefoxitin or oxacillin.</td>
