
               
               
               7 DRUG INTERACTIONS
               
               
                  
                     
                     
                     7.1 Ciprofloxacin
                     
                        Co-administration of 40 mg/kg raxibacumab IV with IV or oral ciprofloxacin in human subjects did not alter the PK of either ciprofloxacin or raxibacumab [see Clinical Pharmacology (12.3)].
