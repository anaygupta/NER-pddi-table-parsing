
               
               
               CLINICAL PHARMACOLOGY
               
                  Aminosyn 10% (pH 6), Sulfite-Free, (an amino acid
injection) provides amino acids to promote protein synthesis and wound
healing, and to reduce the rate of endogenous protein catabolism.
Aminosyn 10% (pH 6), given by central venous infusion in combination
with concentrated dextrose, electrolytes, vitamins, trace metals,
and ancillary fat supplements, constitutes total parenteral nutrition
(TPN). Aminosyn 10% (pH 6) can also be administered by peripheral
vein with dextrose and maintenance electrolytes. Intravenous fat emulsion
may be substituted for part of the carbohydrate calories during either
TPN or peripheral vein administration of Aminosyn 10% (pHÂ 6).
