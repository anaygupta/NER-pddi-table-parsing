                              WARNINGS
                           
                           
                              PRECAUTIONS
                           
                        
                        Methocarbamol may inhibit the effect of pyridostigmine bromide. Therefore, methocarbamol should be used with caution in patients with myasthenia gravis receiving anticholinesterase agents.
