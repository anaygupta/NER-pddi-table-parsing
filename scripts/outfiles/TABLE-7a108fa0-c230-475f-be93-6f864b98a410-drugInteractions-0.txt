<tbody>
<tr valign="top">
<td stylecode="Toprule Botrule Rrule Lrule">Adenosine</td>
<td stylecode="Toprule Botrule Rrule Lrule">Theophylline blocks adenosine
														<br/>receptors.</td>
<td stylecode="Toprule Botrule Rrule Lrule">Higher doses of adenosine may be required to achieve
														<br/>desired effect.</td>
</tr>
<tr valign="top">
<td stylecode="Toprule Botrule Rrule Lrule">Alcohol</td>
<td stylecode="Toprule Botrule Rrule Lrule">A single large dose of alcohol (3
														<br/>mL/kg of whiskey) decreases
														<br/>theophylline clearance for up to
														<br/>24 hours.</td>
<td stylecode="Toprule Botrule Rrule Lrule">30% increase</td>
</tr>
<tr valign="top">
<td stylecode="Toprule Botrule Rrule Lrule">Allopurinol</td>
<td stylecode="Toprule Botrule Rrule Lrule">Decreases theophylline clearance
														<br/>at allopurinol doses ≥600 mg/day.</td>
<td stylecode="Toprule Botrule Rrule Lrule">25% increase</td>
</tr>
<tr valign="top">
<td stylecode="Toprule Botrule Rrule Lrule">Aminoglutethimide</td>
<td stylecode="Toprule Botrule Rrule Lrule">Increases theophylline clearance
														<br/>by induction of microsomal
														<br/>enzyme activity.</td>
<td stylecode="Toprule Botrule Rrule Lrule">25% decrease</td>
</tr>
<tr valign="top">
<td stylecode="Toprule Botrule Rrule Lrule">Carbamazepine</td>
<td stylecode="Toprule Botrule Rrule Lrule">Similar to aminoglutethimide.</td>
<td stylecode="Toprule Botrule Rrule Lrule">30% decrease</td>
</tr>
<tr valign="top">
<td stylecode="Toprule Botrule Rrule Lrule">Cimetidine</td>
<td stylecode="Toprule Botrule Rrule Lrule">Decreases theophylline clearance
														<br/>by inhibiting cytochrome P450
														<br/>1A2.</td>
<td stylecode="Toprule Botrule Rrule Lrule">70% increase</td>
</tr>
<tr valign="top">
<td stylecode="Toprule Botrule Rrule Lrule">Ciprofloxacin</td>
<td stylecode="Toprule Botrule Rrule Lrule">Similar to cimetidine.</td>
<td stylecode="Toprule Botrule Rrule Lrule">40% increase</td>
</tr>
<tr valign="top">
<td stylecode="Toprule Botrule Rrule Lrule">Clarithromycin</td>
<td stylecode="Toprule Botrule Rrule Lrule">Similar to erythromycin.</td>
<td stylecode="Toprule Botrule Rrule Lrule">25% increase</td>
</tr>
<tr valign="top">
<td stylecode="Toprule Botrule Rrule Lrule">Diazepam</td>
<td stylecode="Toprule Botrule Rrule Lrule">Benzodiazepines increase CNS
														<br/>concentrations of adenosine, a
														<br/>potent CNS depressant, while
														<br/>theophylline blocks adenosine
														<br/>receptors.</td>
<td stylecode="Toprule Botrule Rrule Lrule">Larger diazepam doses may be required to produce
														<br/>desired level of sedation. Discontinuation of
														<br/>theophylline without reduction of diazepam dose may
														<br/>result in respiratory depression.</td>
</tr>
<tr valign="top">
<td stylecode="Toprule Botrule Rrule Lrule">Disulfiram</td>
<td stylecode="Toprule Botrule Rrule Lrule">Decreases theophylline clearance
														<br/>by inhibiting hydroxylation and
														<br/>demethylation.</td>
<td stylecode="Toprule Botrule Rrule Lrule">50% increase</td>
</tr>
<tr valign="top">
<td stylecode="Toprule Botrule Rrule Lrule">Enoxacin</td>
<td stylecode="Toprule Botrule Rrule Lrule">Similar to cimetidine.</td>
<td stylecode="Toprule Botrule Rrule Lrule">300% increase</td>
</tr>
<tr valign="top">
<td stylecode="Toprule Botrule Rrule Lrule">Ephedrine</td>
<td stylecode="Toprule Botrule Rrule Lrule">Synergistic CNS effects.</td>
<td stylecode="Toprule Botrule Rrule Lrule">Increased frequency of nausea, nervousness, and
														<br/>insomnia.</td>
</tr>
<tr valign="top">
<td stylecode="Toprule Botrule Rrule Lrule">Erythromycin</td>
<td stylecode="Toprule Botrule Rrule Lrule">Erythromycin metabolite
														<br/>decreases theophylline clearance
														<br/>by inhibiting cytochrome P450
														<br/>3A3.</td>
<td stylecode="Toprule Botrule Rrule Lrule">35% increase. Erythromycin steady-state serum
														<br/>concentrations decrease by a similar amount.</td>
</tr>
<tr valign="top">
<td stylecode="Toprule Botrule Rrule Lrule">Estrogen</td>
<td stylecode="Toprule Botrule Rrule Lrule">Estrogen containing oral
														<br/>contraceptives decrease
														<br/>theophylline clearance in a dose-
														<br/>dependent fashion. The effect of
														<br/>progesterone on theophylline
														<br/>clearance is unknown.</td>
<td stylecode="Toprule Botrule Rrule Lrule" valign="middle">30% increase</td>
</tr>
<tr valign="top">
<td stylecode="Toprule Botrule Rrule Lrule">Flurazepam</td>
<td stylecode="Toprule Botrule Rrule Lrule">Similar to diazepam.</td>
<td stylecode="Toprule Botrule Rrule Lrule">Similar to diazepam.</td>
</tr>
<tr valign="top">
<td stylecode="Toprule Botrule Rrule Lrule">Fluvoxamine</td>
<td stylecode="Toprule Botrule Rrule Lrule">Similar to cimetidine.</td>
<td stylecode="Toprule Botrule Rrule Lrule">Similar to cimetidine</td>
</tr>
<tr valign="top">
<td stylecode="Toprule Botrule Rrule Lrule">Halothane</td>
<td stylecode="Toprule Botrule Rrule Lrule">Halothane sensitizes the
														<br/>myocardium to catecholamines,
														<br/>theophylline increases release of
														<br/>endogenous catecholamines.</td>
<td stylecode="Toprule Botrule Rrule Lrule">Increased risk of ventricular arrhythmias.</td>
</tr>
<tr valign="top">
<td stylecode="Toprule Botrule Rrule Lrule">Interferon, human
														<br/>recombinant alpha-A</td>
<td stylecode="Toprule Botrule Rrule Lrule">Decreases theophylline clearance.</td>
<td stylecode="Toprule Botrule Rrule Lrule">100% increase</td>
</tr>
<tr valign="top">
<td stylecode="Toprule Botrule Rrule Lrule">Isoproterenol (IV)</td>
<td stylecode="Toprule Botrule Rrule Lrule">Increases theophylline clearance.</td>
<td stylecode="Toprule Botrule Rrule Lrule">20% decrease</td>
</tr>
<tr valign="top">
<td stylecode="Toprule Botrule Rrule Lrule">Ketamine</td>
<td stylecode="Toprule Botrule Rrule Lrule">Pharmacologic.</td>
<td stylecode="Toprule Botrule Rrule Lrule">May lower theophylline seizure threshold.</td>
</tr>
<tr valign="top">
<td stylecode="Toprule Botrule Rrule Lrule">Lithium</td>
<td stylecode="Toprule Botrule Rrule Lrule">Theophylline increases renal
														<br/>lithium clearance.</td>
<td stylecode="Toprule Botrule Rrule Lrule">Lithium dose required to achieve a therapeutic serum
														<br/>concentration increased an average of 60%.</td>
</tr>
<tr valign="top">
<td stylecode="Toprule Botrule Rrule Lrule">Lorazepam</td>
<td stylecode="Toprule Botrule Rrule Lrule">Similar to diazepam.</td>
<td stylecode="Toprule Botrule Rrule Lrule">Similar to diazepam.</td>
</tr>
<tr valign="top">
<td stylecode="Toprule Botrule Rrule Lrule">Methotrexate (MTX)</td>
<td stylecode="Toprule Botrule Rrule Lrule">Decreases theophylline clearance.</td>
<td stylecode="Toprule Botrule Rrule Lrule">20% increase after low dose MTX, higher dose MTX
														<br/>may have a greater effect.</td>
</tr>
<tr valign="top">
<td stylecode="Toprule Botrule Rrule Lrule">Mexiletine</td>
<td stylecode="Toprule Botrule Rrule Lrule">Similar to disulfiram.</td>
<td stylecode="Toprule Botrule Rrule Lrule">80% increase</td>
</tr>
<tr valign="top">
<td stylecode="Toprule Botrule Rrule Lrule">Midazolam</td>
<td stylecode="Toprule Botrule Rrule Lrule">Similar to diazepam.</td>
<td stylecode="Toprule Botrule Rrule Lrule">Similar to diazepam.</td>
</tr>
<tr valign="top">
<td stylecode="Toprule Botrule Rrule Lrule">Moricizine</td>
<td stylecode="Toprule Botrule Rrule Lrule">Increases theophylline clearance.</td>
<td stylecode="Toprule Botrule Rrule Lrule">25% decrease</td>
</tr>
<tr valign="top">
<td stylecode="Toprule Botrule Rrule Lrule">Pancuronium</td>
<td stylecode="Toprule Botrule Rrule Lrule">Theophylline may antagonize
														<br/>non-depolarizing neuromuscular
														<br/>blocking effects, possibly due to
														<br/>phosphodiesterase inhibition.</td>
<td stylecode="Toprule Botrule Rrule Lrule">Larger dose of pancuronium may be required to
														<br/>achieve neuromuscular blockade</td>
</tr>
<tr valign="top">
<td stylecode="Toprule Botrule Rrule Lrule">Pentoxifylline</td>
<td stylecode="Toprule Botrule Rrule Lrule">Decreases theophylline clearance.</td>
<td stylecode="Toprule Botrule Rrule Lrule">30% increase</td>
</tr>
<tr valign="top">
<td stylecode="Toprule Botrule Rrule Lrule">Phenobarbital (PB)</td>
<td stylecode="Toprule Botrule Rrule Lrule">Similar to aminoglutethimide.</td>
<td stylecode="Toprule Botrule Rrule Lrule">25% decrease after two weeks of concurrent PB.</td>
</tr>
<tr valign="top">
<td stylecode="Toprule Botrule Rrule Lrule">Phenytoin</td>
<td stylecode="Toprule Botrule Rrule Lrule">Phenytoin increases theophylline
														<br/>clearance by increasing
														<br/>microsomal enzyme activity.
														<br/>Theophylline decreases
														<br/>phenytoin absorption.</td>
<td stylecode="Toprule Botrule Rrule Lrule">Serum theophylline <content stylecode="underline">and</content> phenytoin concentrations
														<br/>decrease about 40%.</td>
</tr>
<tr valign="top">
<td stylecode="Toprule Botrule Rrule Lrule">Propafenone</td>
<td stylecode="Toprule Botrule Rrule Lrule">Decreases theophylline clearance
														<br/>and pharmacologic interaction.</td>
<td stylecode="Toprule Botrule Rrule Lrule">40% increase. Beta<sub>2</sub> blocking effect may decrease
														<br/>efficacy of theophylline</td>
</tr>
<tr valign="top">
<td stylecode="Toprule Botrule Rrule Lrule">Propranolol</td>
<td stylecode="Toprule Botrule Rrule Lrule">Similar to cimetidine and
														<br/>pharmacologic interaction.</td>
<td stylecode="Toprule Botrule Rrule Lrule">100% increase. Beta<sub>2</sub> blocking effect may decrease
														<br/>efficacy of theophylline</td>
</tr>
<tr valign="top">
<td stylecode="Toprule Botrule Rrule Lrule">Rifampin</td>
<td stylecode="Toprule Botrule Rrule Lrule">Increases theophylline clearance
														<br/>by increasing cytochrome P450
														<br/>1A2 and 3A3 activity.</td>
<td stylecode="Toprule Botrule Rrule Lrule">20-40% decrease</td>
</tr>
<tr valign="top">
<td stylecode="Toprule Botrule Rrule Lrule">St. John's Wort
														<br/>(Hypericum
														<br/>Perforatum)</td>
<td stylecode="Toprule Botrule Rrule Lrule">Decrease in theophylline plasma
														<br/>concentrations.</td>
<td stylecode="Toprule Botrule Rrule Lrule">Higher doses of theophylline may be required to
														<br/>achieve desired effect. Stopping St. John's Wort may
														<br/>result in theophylline toxicity.</td>
</tr>
<tr valign="top">
<td stylecode="Toprule Botrule Rrule Lrule">Sulfinpyrazone</td>
<td stylecode="Toprule Botrule Rrule Lrule">Increases theophylline clearance
														<br/>by increasing demethylation and
														<br/>hydroxylation. Decreases renal
														<br/>clearance of theophylline.</td>
<td stylecode="Toprule Botrule Rrule Lrule">20% decrease</td>
</tr>
<tr valign="top">
<td stylecode="Toprule Botrule Rrule Lrule">Tacrine</td>
<td stylecode="Toprule Botrule Rrule Lrule">Similar to cimetidine, also
														<br/>increases renal clearance of
														<br/>theophylline.</td>
<td stylecode="Toprule Botrule Rrule Lrule">90% increase</td>
</tr>
<tr valign="top">
<td stylecode="Toprule Botrule Rrule Lrule">Thiabendazole</td>
<td stylecode="Toprule Botrule Rrule Lrule">Decreases theophylline clearance.</td>
<td stylecode="Toprule Botrule Rrule Lrule">190% increase</td>
</tr>
<tr valign="top">
<td stylecode="Toprule Botrule Rrule Lrule">Ticlopidine</td>
<td stylecode="Toprule Botrule Rrule Lrule">Decreases theophylline clearance.</td>
<td stylecode="Toprule Botrule Rrule Lrule">60% increase</td>
</tr>
<tr valign="top">
<td stylecode="Toprule Botrule Rrule Lrule">Troleandomycin</td>
<td stylecode="Toprule Botrule Rrule Lrule">Similar to erythromycin.</td>
<td stylecode="Toprule Botrule Rrule Lrule">33-100% increase depending on troleandomycin dose.</td>
</tr>
<tr valign="top">
<td stylecode="Toprule Botrule Rrule Lrule">Verapamil</td>
<td stylecode="Toprule Botrule Rrule Lrule">Similar to disulfiram.</td>
<td stylecode="Toprule Botrule Rrule Lrule">20% increase</td>
