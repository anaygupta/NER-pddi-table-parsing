
                     
                     
                     Drug-Food Interaction
                     
                        In all clinical trials, patients were instructed to take Bexarotene capsules with or immediately following a meal. In one clinical study, plasma bexarotene AUC and Cmax values were substantially higher following a fat-containing meal versus those following the administration of a glucose solution. Because safety and efficacy data are based upon administration with food, it is recommended that Bexarotene capsules be administered with food (see 
                              CLINICAL PHARMACOLOGY: Pharmacokinetics and DOSAGE AND ADMINISTRATION
                           ).
