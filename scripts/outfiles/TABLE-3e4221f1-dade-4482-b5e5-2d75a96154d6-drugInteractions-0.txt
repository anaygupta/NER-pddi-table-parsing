<td align="left" valign="top">Diazepam</td>
<td align="left" valign="top">Benzodiazepines increase
                                                CNS concentrations of adenosine, a potent CNS
                                                depressant, while theophylline blocks adenosine
                                                receptors.</td>
<td align="left" valign="top">Larger diazepam doses may
                                                be required to produce desired level of sedation.
                                                Discontinuation of theophylline without reduction of
                                                diazepam dose may result in respiratory
                                                depression.</td>
</tr>
