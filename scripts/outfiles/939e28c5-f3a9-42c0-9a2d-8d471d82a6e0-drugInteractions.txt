
               
               
               7 DRUG INTERACTIONS
               
               
                  
                     
                     
                     7.1 Dopamine Antagonists
                     
                        Dopamine antagonists, such as antipsychotics or metoclopramide, may diminish the effectiveness of NEUPRO [see Clinical Pharmacology (12.3)].
